What the tools do

✏️ Inspect reads a Google Doc's revision history — writing sessions, pastes, struggle moments. Free, no account.
🔍 StyleMatch compares a controlled sample with submitted work across 8 stylometric metrics, entirely on your device.
🔬 Verify runs AI-assisted consistency analysis and produces a documented report.

None of them are AI detectors, and none of them issue verdicts. They give you a structured, repeatable, documentable data point — the judgment stays with the educator.

Frequently asked

The sidebar says I don't have access to the revision history (403).
Inspect needs Editor access to the Google Doc. Viewer or Commenter access can't read the revision API — ask the student to share the document with edit rights, or open it from Google Classroom where you own the assignment copy.
Does PaperTrail for Word have Inspect?
No — Microsoft Word doesn't expose a revision changelog the way Google Docs does, so Inspect is technically impossible there. StyleMatch and Verify work fully in Word. For revision-history analysis, use the Chrome extension with Google Docs.
Where does student text go?
StyleMatch runs entirely in your browser or in Word on your machine — no text is transmitted. Verify transmits text to our server for AI analysis and does not retain it after the report is generated. Details in the privacy policy.
One account, three surfaces?
Yes. The same PaperTrail account works in the Chrome extension, in PaperTrail for Word, and on this site — subscription and tokens carry over. Sign in with email, Google, or Clever.
How do subscriptions and tokens work?
StyleMatch Plus is a subscription; Verify and Oral Defense draw from a shared wallet of PaperTrail Tokens that never expire. Purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy (our merchant of record); manage or cancel any time at the billing portal.
My purchase hasn't shown up.
Credits usually land within a minute. If they don't: sign out and back in, then email us with the receipt — we'll fix it same day.
My school uses Clever.
Teachers can log in with Clever wherever PaperTrail offers sign-in. Districts interested in wider rollout: see For Schools or email us.

Data & privacy requests
