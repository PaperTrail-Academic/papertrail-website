Our Mission

Bringing confidence back to writing done outside the classroom

Something changed. Teachers who spent careers helping students find their voice now spend energy wondering whether the work in front of them actually belongs to the student who submitted it. AI tools, contract cheating, and simple copy-paste have made that question harder to answer — and harder to document.

PaperTrail Academic exists for that moment of doubt. Not to accuse. Not to replace teacher judgment. But to give teachers a structured, repeatable, documentable way to look at the evidence — and to have a fair, defensible conversation with a student, a parent, or an administrator.

Every tool we build is guided by one principle: the evidence should speak clearly, and the judgment should stay yours.

PaperTrail Inspect

See how it was really written

Everything you need to understand a student's writing process — installed in seconds, no account required.

📅

Full Revision Timeline

Every edit, session, and pause — laid out chronologically. See exactly when work happened, how long it took, and how the document evolved from first keystroke to final submission.

📋

Paste Event Detection

Every paste event is flagged with a timestamp, character count, and novelty score — showing what percentage of the pasted text was new to the document. High novelty suggests external sourcing.

⚡

Struggle Moments

Regions of heavy revision — repeated rewrites, deletions, and reworking — are automatically detected and flagged. These are among the strongest signals of genuine engagement with the writing process.

▶️

Revision Playback

Watch the document being written from the very first character to the final version — like looking over the student's shoulder in real time. Pause, rewind, and jump to any moment.

🔎

Paste Comparison

For every paste event, see the document state before and after — side by side. Understand exactly what was replaced, inserted, or added from an external source.

📊

Deep Writing Metrics

Deletion ratio, revision density, writing session breakdown, and activity flags — a full analytical picture of how the document was built, not just what it looks like now.

Struggle Moments

⚡ Where the real writing happened

Struggle moments are regions where a student revised heavily — high deletion, rewriting, or repeated edits across a short span of time. They're one of the strongest signals of genuine engagement with the writing process.

PaperTrail automatically identifies these regions and marks them on the writing timeline. Click any ⚡ bolt in playback to jump directly to that moment and watch the revision unfold in real time.

Paste Comparison

See exactly what changed

When a paste event is detected, PaperTrail shows you exactly what was pasted, when, and how novel that text is relative to the rest of the document. A high novelty score means most pasted words were new to the document — a strong signal of external sourcing.

The paste comparison viewer shows the document before and after each event, side by side, so you can see precisely what was replaced or inserted.
